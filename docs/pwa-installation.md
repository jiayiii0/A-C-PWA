# PWA Installation Guide

## iPhone and iPad

1. Open the deployed app in Safari.
2. Tap the Share icon.
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Confirm the name **Housely A/C**.

## Android

1. Open the deployed app in Chrome.
2. Tap **Install app** if prompted, or open the menu and tap **Add to Home screen**.
3. Confirm installation.

The app uses a light theme, mobile bottom navigation, and a cached offline page.
